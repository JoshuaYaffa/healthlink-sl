About HealthLink SL

Your Health, Our Priority

Our Mission

To improve health and save lives by connecting people in Sierra Leone to healthcare services, information, and emergency support through accessible digital technology.

Our Vision

A Sierra Leone where every citizen has instant access to quality healthcare information and emergency services, regardless of their location or internet connectivity.

The Problem We're Solving

Healthcare accessibility remains a major challenge in Sierra Leone, especially in rural communities. Many people face difficulties locating nearby clinics, accessing emergency services, or finding reliable health information. Delays in medical assistance often lead to preventable deaths.
