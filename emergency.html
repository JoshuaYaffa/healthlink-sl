What to Do While Waiting?

Push hard and fast in the center of the chest at 100-120 compressions per minute. Call for help immediately.

Apply direct pressure to the wound with a clean cloth. Elevate the injured area if possible. Do not remove embedded objects.

Keep the person cool with a damp cloth. Encourage fluids. Seek medical attention if fever is high or persistent.
